# 💎 Distilled Principles

The durable ideas as they accumulate, tagged by source part. Re-read these weekly.

## From Part 1 — The Ultimate Success Formula

1. **Personal power = the ability to act.** Information doesn't change your life; decisions and action do.
2. **The past does not equal the future** — this moment is a clean start.
3. **The Ultimate Success Formula:** (1) know your outcome, (2) take action, (3) notice what you're getting, (4) change your approach until it works.
4. **Accelerator — model people already getting the result.** Success leaves clues: copy the *process*, not just the outcome.
5. **Clarity is power. Flexibility is power.**
6. **To "decide" means to cut off from** every other option — then act at once.
7. **Knowledge is only *potential* power.**
8. **There is no failure, only feedback.** Frustration usually means a breakthrough is near.
9. **Small daily improvements compound.** Major in the majors; don't sweat the minors.
10. **Watch the comfort-zone set-point** — after a win, we tend to self-sabotage back to what we secretly think we deserve.
11. **Repetition is the mother of skill.**

## From Part 2 — Pain &amp; Pleasure

12. **Pain and pleasure are the controlling force** of all behavior — we act to avoid pain or gain pleasure.
13. **We'll do more to avoid pain than to gain pleasure** (loss aversion). Most people don't change until staying the same hurts too much — their "threshold."
14. **Procrastination = linking more pain to acting than to not acting.** Flip the scale: focus on the cost of *not* doing it.
15. **Don't rely on willpower — re-wire the associations.** Change what you link pain and pleasure to, rather than gritting through discipline.
16. **Reality is whatever you focus on.** Shift your focus and you change which feeling — pain or pleasure — drives you right now.
17. **Use the carrot and the stick on yourself, deliberately** — or advertisers and circumstances will do it for you.

## From Part 3 — Neuro-Associations

- **The meaning you link to something — not the event — drives your behavior.**
- We live in a stimulus–response world; **change the conditioned response, not just your thoughts** (positive thinking alone isn't enough).
- **Strong emotion installs associations fast** — intensity and repetition are how conditioning sticks.

## From Part 4 — Neuro-Associative Conditioning (NAC)

- **Change = get leverage → interrupt the pattern → condition a new association.**
- "Should" and "could" don't move you; **"must" does.**
- Lasting change is **daily conditioning, not a one-time fix** (keep tuning the piano).

## From Part 5 — Managing Your State

- **You don't want the thing, you want the feeling** — managing your state is the master skill.
- **Emotion is created by motion:** change your body (posture, breath, face) to change how you feel.
- **Performance reflects state, not ability** — and you control your state.

## From Part 8 — The Power of Focus

- **Whatever you focus on is what you feel;** the brain deletes everything else.
- **Your habitual questions steer your focus** — ask better questions, get better states and answers.
- A **morning ritual of empowering questions** primes the whole day.

## From Part 9 — Values &amp; Beliefs

- **Values are the ranked feelings you move toward or away from** — they silently steer every choice.
- **Beliefs are if/then rules** that decide whether you've "hit" a value; most people's rules make winning impossible.
- **Conflicting values** (wanting success *and* comfort) cause self-sabotage.

## From Part 10 — Changing Core Beliefs

- **Judge a belief by whether it's useful, not whether it's "true."**
- **Beliefs are just generalizations** — they can be re-edited.
- **The Dickens pattern:** feel the future pain of a belief, then the pleasure of its replacement, to flip it.

## From Part 11 — The Power of Goal Setting

- **Purpose beats outcome:** the *why* pulls harder than the *what*.
- **Reasons come first, answers come second.**
- **Use dissatisfaction and self-imposed pressure as fuel.**

## From Part 12 — The Goal-Setting Workshop

- **Goals are dreams with a deadline** — brainstorm without limits, then assign timelines.
- **Never leave the site of a goal without taking one action** toward it.
- **Keep your top goals visible and review them daily.**

## From Part 15 — Rituals

- **Every emotion is a repeatable recipe** of body + focus — find the recipe, change the result.
- **Environmental anchors auto-trigger your rituals** — design better triggers.
- **Never make an emotion your identity** — "I'm running a ritual," not "I am a failure."

## From Part 16 — Anchoring

- **You're always anchoring;** the skill is doing it consciously.
- **Intense state + a unique, repeated trigger = an installed anchor.**
- **Set the trigger at the peak** of the feeling; behavior follows state.

## From Part 17 — The Financial Genius Within

- **Wealth is conditioning + strategy, not luck** — make abundance a *must*.
- **Passion without a proven strategy fails** — model people who built wealth.
- **What you don't use, you lose;** gratitude is the essence of real wealth.

## From Part 18 — End Financial Self-Sabotage

- **Self-sabotage (mixed money associations), not lack of a plan, is the real block.**
- **Surface and ridicule the limiting money beliefs you inherited.**
- **Condition wealth daily;** give, save, and invest a fixed slice of every dollar.

## From Part 19 — Conquering Fear

- **Failure, success, and rejection are labels set by your own rules** — rewrite the rules.
- **FEAR is often False Evidence Appearing Real.**
- **Massive action through rejection is the path;** erase the sting of past "failures."

## From Part 22 — Unstoppable Self-Confidence

- **Confidence is a state you switch on** (physiology + focus + belief), not a fixed trait.
- **Expect** (one vivid picture), don't merely **hope** (dim and passive).
- **Bank your past wins as proof;** rehearse success in advance.

## From Part 23 — Eat Live Foods (Health)

- **The quality of your life equals the quality of your cells.**
- **Eat live, water-rich foods** (aim ~70%); let thirst guide drinking.
- **Every disciplined effort pays a multiple reward.**

## From Part 24 — Successful Relationships

- **The quality of your life is the quality of your relationships.**
- **Come to give, not to take** — what you put out returns multiplied.
- **Keep arguments short, never let pain stack to threshold,** and learn your partner's love strategy.

## From Part 25 — Solving Problems Fast

- **Spend 80% on the solution, 20% on the problem.**
- **Reframe "problems" as "challenges"** — words create states.
- **Ask empowering questions;** problems exist to grow you.

## From Part 26 — Mastery &amp; CANI

- **Choose mastery over dabbling or stressing** — push *through* the plateau.
- **CANI — Constant And Never-ending Improvement** — practice fundamentals daily.
- **Beware the "disease of success"** (the groove becomes a grave); fulfillment comes from who you *become* and what you *contribute*.
