# ⚡ Personal Power — Field Guide

A living companion to Tony Robbins' **Personal Power** (a 30-part program, originally released 1989). Each part gets a cleaned transcript and a summary, and the timeless ideas accumulate into a principles page and an action tracker — so this becomes something you *revisit and act on*, not notes you read once.

> **The one idea this whole program rests on:** what changes your life is not learning more — it's making decisions and taking action. *The past does not equal the future.*

## How this guide is organized

- **[Distilled principles](guide/principles.md)** — the durable ideas, stripped of the era's sales pitch, tagged by the part they came from.
- **[Action &amp; habit tracker](guide/tracker.md)** — every exercise from every part, in one checklist.
- **Days** — one summary per part (in the sidebar), each linking to its full cleaned transcript.

## How to get lasting change out of it

1. Do the day's exercise the **same day** you read the summary — the program runs on action, not information.
2. Keep the **tracker** visible and check things off.
3. Re-read the **principles** weekly. Repetition is the mother of skill.
4. Keep a **Success Journal** (Robbins' Day 1 assignment) for your own reflections alongside this guide.
